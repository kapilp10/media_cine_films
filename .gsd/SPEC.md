# SPEC.md — MediaCine Films Website

> **Status**: FINALIZED

## Vision
A beautiful, highly-aesthetic web presence for "MediaCine Films," a wedding cinematography business. The site will closely mirror the clean, elegant, whitespace-heavy design of the Nakula Framer template while delivering modern functionality to handle client inquiries, display services, and manage bookings.

## Goals
1. Establish a breathtaking online portfolio to showcase wedding cinematography ("Works").
2. Clearly articulate service offerings and "Pricing Packages".
3. Streamline the client acquisition and management process via a "Booking Calendar" and "Client Portal".

## Non-Goals (Out of Scope)
- Elaborate e-commerce functionality outside of simple service/package booking.
- Complex user social networking features.

## Users
- engaged couples seeking premium wedding cinematography.
- prospective clients wanting to view packages, check availability, and make bookings.
- existing clients logging into a portal to perhaps view their contracts or final delivered media.

## Constraints
- **Tech Stack**: Next.js (App Router) and Tailwind CSS.
- **Aesthetic**: Must strictly adhere to the Nakula Framer template vibe (minimalist, sophisticated typography, smooth interactions).
- **Core Pages**: Home, Works, About, Contact, Services.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Next.js project is successfully initialized with Tailwind CSS.
- [ ] All 5 core pages have their scaffolding established.
- [ ] The aesthetic (typography, spacing, color palette) undeniably matches the Nakula reference.
- [ ] MVP versions of the Booking Calendar, Pricing Packages, and Client Portal are implemented.
